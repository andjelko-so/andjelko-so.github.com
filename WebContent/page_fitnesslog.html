FitnessLog

Our main target is keeping strong presence on mobile market, and developing iOS applications is primary task. One our first iOS applications is FitnessLog, iPhone application for gym users, which enables them to create daily program, keep evidence of their daily activities and progress, visualize their results using progress graphs... With our application, you can in single click connect your trainer's iPad, and download training program.

Along with this, FitnessLog application have large library of example exercises, followed with images, description of usage, sample trainings for different categories of users, estimated calorie burn for sample trainings. Although this library is large, we are aware that needs of each individual user could spread indefinitely. Therefore, you can always easily add more and more exercises to the library.



Since you always carry your phone with you, you need to be able to personalize your application. That's why, we have added ability for you to enter personal details, and, of course, to connect your application to the Facebook profile. Based on this personal data, you can calculate BMI and ideal weight.
